    Story

    For over a decade, we’ve been motivated by our belief - that the world we want for our children starts with how we do business today.

    Polar Bear – The Sundae Ice Cream Zone has evolved to create products and
    services that people love. Our story began when the founder, Kishore Rai
    discovered his love for ice cream and created much-loved sundaes that stood the
    test of time.

    OUR APPROACH

    We have worked on a clear direction and a moral compass for all of our people working across 100+ outlets. The long-term future of our business and the planet depends on us to stand for more and take bold action. That is why we take every opportunity to make a positive impact in this ever-changing world.

    QUALITY

    We are committed to quality, because how our products are made matters to our customers, communities, and the environment.

    TRANSPARENCY

    Transparency is woven into our DNA. We demonstrate honesty, accountability, and a genuine attitude in all business interactions.

    CUSTOMER DELIGHT

    We listen to our customers and create delightful experiences by surpassing their expectations with our products and services.

    RESPONSIBILITY

    For us, responsibility is all about ownership and clarity. We are responsible for the products that we provide to our customers.

    RESPECT

    At Polar Bear, we show respect in the way we treat each other; the way we treat those we serve.

    Within the organization, we use people's ideas to change or improve the workplace. We embrace everyone for their uniqueness.

    WHAT OUR LEADERS HAVE TO SAY!

    KISHORE KUMAR RAI
    FOUNDER


    Polar Bear, the Ice Cream Sundae Zone, began its journey in the year 2008, with the first-ever parlour set up at ITPL in Bangalore.

    When we stepped into the market, the ice-cream industry in India was booming. We spent a lot of time on research and trying to understand the Indian audience. We created new and existing flavours, gave a makeover to the age-old sundae recipes, and made magic that appealed to the varied tastes of the diverse Indian customer. Our ice creams are 100% dairy and 100% veg, and are made with the best ingredients, in the best facility, with utmost hygiene and lots of love.


    PRASHANTH PRAKASH
    GENERAL MANAGER - OPERATIONS


    Ice cream is the go-to dessert for Indians after a wholesome meal. We tapped on the everlasting Indian craving for ice creams to create an unforgettable ice cream experience for our customers. We offer 70+ natural and exotic flavours in our lip-smacking range of ice-cream sundaes, shakes, cakes, and more, at our vibrant and cheerful outlets.

    Little did we know that our brand will be cherished and widely recognized by the Indian audience within a short span of 13 years!
