    OUR PLANET MATTERS

    WE ACT IN WAYS TO CREATE A MORE SUSTAINABLE AND BETTER SHARED FUTURE. TO MAKE A
    DIFFERENCE IN PEOPLE'S LIVES, COMMUNITIES AND OUR PLANET BY DOING BUSINESS THE RIGHT WAY.




    We continue to prioritize our sustainability goals as the business emerges stronger from the global pandemic while constantly focusing our energy
    and resources where our products can make the greatest difference to the lives of customers and communities, protect and enhance the
    environment, and generate significant value for our shareholders and stakeholders alike.



    ALIGNING OURSELVES WITH
    THE UNITED NATIONS'
    SUSTAINABLE
    DEVELOPMENT GOALS.




    NO GREENHOUSE GASES IN REFRIGERATION UNIT

    At Polar Bear, we have worked towards indulging in our guilty pleasure with a clear green conscience? We have made a switch to green refrigeration and better insulation in ice-cream freezers. This effort from our side will help to slash greenhouse gas emissions.




    REDUCING OUR CARBON FOOTPRINT

    We have moved to using complete LED lighting in all our outlets. With an estimated energy efficiency of 80-90% compared to conventional light bulbs, LEDs significantly reduce energy consumption, thus making them environmentally sustainable.




    GIVING OUR CUSTOMERS OPTIONS AND CHOICES

    We are evolving our business strategy to become a total Sundae company by giving people more of the choices they want. This includes innovating newer recipes, reducing added sugar products, adding natural range, using fresh fruits in our sundaes, and making different packaging sizes more available to enable portion control.




    RETHINKING PACKAGING

    We recognize our responsibility to help solve the global plastic waste crisis. Our ambitious sustainable packaging initiative aims to create systemic change for our packaging, by using paper bags, paper cups, and glass bottles for Takeaway. We will slowly move into more sustainable ways of delivering our products.




    IMPROVING THE LIFE OF OUR COMMUNITY

    At Polar Bear, we truly believe that everyone working within our extended supply chains should earn a sufficient income to maintain a decent standard of living.
